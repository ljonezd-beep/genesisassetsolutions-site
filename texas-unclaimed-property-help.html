Texas Unclaimed Property Help

The Texas Comptroller currently holds billions of dollars in unclaimed property belonging to businesses and individuals across the state.

Many companies have funds waiting due to:

  • Uncashed vendor or payroll checks
  • Dormant bank accounts
  • Insurance refunds
  • Utility deposits
  • Overpayments or escrow balances

Genesis Asset Solutions LLC assists businesses and individuals with researching and recovering unclaimed property through a compliant, documented claims process.

There is no upfront cost. Fees apply only if funds are successfully recovered.
