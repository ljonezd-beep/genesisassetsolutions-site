Services

Professional support for unclaimed property recovery.

Genesis Asset Solutions LLC is designed as a full-service recovery partner. After a client commits and provides the necessary records, we handle the process from initial claim review through documentation, preparation, coordination, and completion support.

Business Claim Support

End-to-end support for companies, legacy entities, dissolved entities, and operational business claims requiring structured documentation and coordinated handling.

Individual Claim Support

Full-process assistance for individuals and qualifying claimant scenarios, including intake, documentation planning, and recovery workflow management.

Preparation Through Completion

We organize the claim package, manage process flow, and stay involved through completion support so the client has one primary recovery partner from start to finish.

Included

What our process is built to handle

  • Initial property review and viability screening
  • Client onboarding and claimant intake
  • Document checklist development and collection support
  • Claim preparation and process coordination
  • End-to-end recovery support through completion
Important:

Claim approval and payment are determined by the applicable state authority. Approved funds are paid by the state directly to the claimant. Genesis Asset Solutions LLC is not the payor and does not hold client funds.

Best starting point:

If you believe funds may be owed, request a free property check first. That gives us enough information to determine whether a formal next step appears appropriate.

Fee structure: We are compensated only after the client receives funds from the state. Our fee is collected from the client after recovery, not from the state.
