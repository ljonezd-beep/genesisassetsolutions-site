How It Works

A clean, documented process from first review to next-step action.

Our workflow is designed to function as a complete, one-stop recovery process. Once the client commits and provides the necessary records, we handle the matter from beginning to end through completion support.

Initial Property Review

You provide the available details. We review the opportunity and determine whether the claim appears worth pursuing.

Client Commitment and Intake

If the opportunity appears viable and the client elects to move forward, we begin formal intake and identify the records needed to support the claim.

Documentation and Preparation

We organize required records, develop the claim package, and manage the documentation process so the matter is properly prepared.

Completion and Recovery Outcome

We stay involved through completion support. If funds are approved, the state pays the claimant directly, and our fee is collected from the client after the client receives the funds.

What you can expect

  • Clear communication
  • Practical next-step guidance
  • Professional intake and records handling
  • A straightforward, businesslike process

What determines success

Successful recovery depends on claimant eligibility, property status, and the sufficiency of supporting documentation required by the applicable state authority. We coordinate the process, but approval and payment remain with the state.
