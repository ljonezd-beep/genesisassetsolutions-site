End-to-end support:

We are built to function as a one-stop recovery partner. After engagement and document collection, we handle the process from beginning to end. The state pays approved funds directly to the claimant, and our fee is collected from the client only after the client receives the funds.

Step 1

Claim Identification

We review available information, assess apparent claimability, and determine whether the opportunity appears worth pursuing.

Step 2

Full-Service Claim Handling

Once the client commits and provides the required documents, we manage the workflow from intake and documentation through claim preparation and process coordination.

Step 3

Client-Paid After Recovery

Approved funds are paid by the state directly to the claimant. We are paid by the client after the client receives the recovered funds.
